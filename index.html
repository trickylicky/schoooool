Who we are, what we do My School Kindergarten & School is a premium, middle-level, privately owned school that offers first-class education at Pre- and Primary-school levels. The School is situated in a serene, secure and leafy location at Jacobs Estate, about 6 kilometers from Nairobi's CBD. The School offers the Kenyan Competency Based Curriculum (CBC).

HABIBI School Kindergarten & School is a co-ed institution, with two distinct but integrated sections: Pre- and Primary School. Its mission being not only to provide an education of the highest quality, but also to care and nurture the Child in a family-like setting. We create and nurture Stars Since its inception, My School has established a reputation as a most ideal place for preparing the brilliant minds of “Young Scholars”, fostering in them the spirit for excelling and a culture of exemplary discipline and a go-getter attitude. Our aim is to bring out the best in the Child, enabling him or her to realize the inherent STARS status that is always latent within them, and hence… Our Motto “STARS IN THE MAKING
